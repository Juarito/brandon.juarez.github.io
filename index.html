About Me


I am Brandon Juarez

Hi, I'm a dedicated and passionate web developer currently pursuing an Associate's degree in Information Technology & Networking at DeVry University. With a certificate in Website Development already under my belt, I am committed to building innovative and efficient web solutions.

My goal is to become a full-stack web developer with a strong foundation in IT and networking. I aim to apply my skills to build innovative web applications that are not only user-friendly but also secure and efficient. I am particularly interested in projects that involve modern web technologies and provide real-world solutions.

Outside of my professional pursuits in web development and IT, I have a rich and varied set of hobbies and interests. I have been playing the guitar since I was 11 years old, making it a passion I've enjoyed for 36 years. Music continues to be a significant part of my life, and I also enjoy playing the drums. My love for the outdoors takes me to amusement parks, with Worlds of Fun being a particular favorite. I find great joy in playing disc golf and spending time on the water. When I'm not outside, you can often find me engaged in video games or working on various DIY projects. I love building and fixing things, leveraging my extensive collection of tools, including a laser engraver. My two awesome dogs keep me company through all these activities. Currently, I work as a maintenance mechanic at a pork processing factory, a role that aligns with my knack for troubleshooting and repairing. Whether it's enjoying a day at the park or tinkering with a new project, I always find a way to balance work and school with my diverse hobbies.
